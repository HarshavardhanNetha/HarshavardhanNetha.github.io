Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2024

This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines the terms and conditions for the delivery of digital services and products offered by Harshavardhan Netha.

Digital Services Delivery

As most of our offerings are digital services, they are typically delivered electronically through one of the following methods:

  • Email delivery
  • Direct download links
  • Access to cloud-based platforms
  • API access credentials
  • Software licenses

Delivery Timeframes

For digital services:

  • Automated digital deliveries: Immediate to 24 hours after payment confirmation
  • Custom digital work: As per the agreed timeline in the contract or statement of work
  • Consulting services: According to the scheduled appointment time

For physical products (if applicable):

  • Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express shipping: 2-3 business days
  • International shipping: 10-20 business days

Delivery Confirmation

Upon successful delivery of any digital product or service, a confirmation email will be sent to the email address provided during purchase. This email will contain:

  • Confirmation of the delivery
  • Access details or download instructions
  • Order summary
  • Support contact information

Delivery Issues

In case of any issues with the delivery of products or services:

  • Check your spam/junk folder for delivery emails
  • Ensure your email address was entered correctly during purchase
  • Contact our support team at harsha@turntbloke.me if the issue persists

International Deliveries

For digital services, there are no geographical restrictions, and delivery should be prompt regardless of location. For physical products (if applicable), international deliveries may be subject to customs duties and taxes imposed by the destination country.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Shipping & Delivery Policy, please contact us at harsha@turntbloke.me.
